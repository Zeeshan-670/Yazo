Introducing Yazo Clock, the ultimate time tracking software that'll have you clocking in and out faster than you can say "extra guac, please!"

Track Your Staff Hours:

Yazo Clock is designed to work seamlessly with Yazo POS, allowing you to track your staff hours, manage payroll, and optimize your operations with ease. No more guessing how much time your employees spend on different tasks, no more paper timecards and no more "I forgot to clock out" excuses.

Easily Track The Time Of Your Employees:

With Yazo Clock, you can easily track the time your employees spend on different tasks and projects, such as prep work, serving customers, and cleaning up. This information can help you to identify inefficiencies, optimize your staff scheduling, and make data-driven decisions to improve your bottom line.

User Friendly & Easy To Use:

Yazo Clock is user-friendly and easy to use, with a simple interface that allows your employees to clock in and out with just a few clicks. It's also fully customizable, so you can set up different projects and tasks to suit your specific restaurant or cafe needs, like "making coffee" or "avoiding customers."

Optimize Your Staff:

With Yazo Clock, you'll have all the information you need to manage your payroll, optimize your staff scheduling and make informed decisions about your business.

Yazo Clock is also accessible from any device, so your employees can clock in and out from anywhere, even if they're working in the kitchen or on the floor. It also features a mobile app, so your employees can track their time on the go, and avoid clocking in while they're on their break.
