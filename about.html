ABOUT US

About Us

We are Mack and Sunny, the dynamic duo behind Yazo.

We started this business because we were tired of seeing small businesses struggle with outdated technology and clunky systems. We wanted to create something that would make running a business easier and more efficient.

With that in mind, we set out to create the ultimate trifecta of products - Yazo POS, Yazo Clock, and Yazo Eats. Our mission is to help small businesses streamline their operations and impress their customers with cutting-edge technology.

We believe in providing top-notch customer service and support, because let's be real, no one likes being stuck on hold for hours. We also believe in keeping it local, which is why we created Yazo Eats to support small local businesses.

Our team at Yazo is made up of experts in the field who are passionate about helping small businesses succeed. We may be small, but we have big ideas and even bigger hearts.

So whether you're a small business owner looking to upgrade your technology or a customer looking for a seamless ordering experience, Yazo is here for you. We're excited to be a part of your journey to success and to make running a business a whole lot more fun!
