Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q1. How does Yazo Eats work?

    Yazo Eats is an online ordering platform that allows customers to place their food orders directly with their favorite restaurants. Customers can browse menus, place orders, and pay for their food all through our user-friendly website or mobile app.

  • Q2. How does Yazo Eats support local small businesses?

    Yazo Eats partners with local small businesses to provide an online ordering platform for their customers. This helps small businesses reach a wider audience and increase their sales. No need to setup a Tech team for service. They can concentrate on perfecting their recipe.

  • Q3. Is there a fee for using Yazo Eats?

    There is no fee for customers to use Yazo Eats. However, our partner restaurants may charge a small fee for each order placed through our platform.

  • Q4. How do I place an order on Yazo Eats?

    To place an order on Yazo Eats, simply visit our mobile app, select the restaurant you want to order from, browse the menu, and add items to your cart. Once you're ready to order, proceed to checkout, where you can review your order and enter your payment information.

  • Q5. How do I pay for my order on Yazo Eats?

    You can pay for your order on Yazo Eats using a credit or debit card, or through a third-party payment processor such as PayPal or Apple Pay.

  • Q6. How do I track my order on Yazo Eats?

    Once you've placed your order, you'll receive a confirmation message with a tracking link. You can use this link to track the status of your order and see an estimate pickup time.

  • Q7. What types of restaurants are available on Yazo Eats?

    Yazo Eats partners with a variety of restaurants, including fast food, casual dining, food trucks and fine dining establishments.

  • Q8. How long does it take for my order to be delivered?

    Delivery times will vary depending on the restaurant and your location. You will be able to see the estimated delivery time when you place your order.

  • Q9. Can I make changes to my order once it has been placed?

    You can make changes to your order if the restaurant has not yet started preparing it. Contact the restaurant directly to make changes to your order.

  • Q10. What should I do if I have a problem with my order?

    If you have a problem with your order, please contact the restaurant directly. If the problem is not resolved to your satisfaction, please contact Yazo Eats customer support.

  • Q11. What are the technical requirements for using Yazo Eats?

    To use Yazo Eats, you'll need a device with internet access, such as a smartphone, tablet, or computer. Our website and mobile app are compatible with most modern browsers, including Chrome, Firefox, and Safari.

  • Q12. Can I use Yazo Eats offline?

    No, Yazo Eats requires an internet connection to place and track orders.

  • Q13.Is Yazo Eats secure?

    Yes, Yazo Eats uses industry-standard encryption to protect your personal and payment information.

  • Q14. Is Yazo Eats compatible with mobile devices?

    Yes, Yazo Eats is available as a mobile app for both iOS and Android devices.

  • Q15. Can I place an order for delivery instead of pickup?

    No, Yazo Eats allows you to place an order for pickup at participating restaurants. You will have the option delivery in Future.

  • Q16. Is there a limit on the number of items I can order?

    There is no limit on the number of items you can order through Yazo Eats, but availability will vary depending on the restaurant.

  • Q17. Can I save my order for future use?

    Yes, you can save your order as a "favorite" for future use, saving you time from having to re-enter your order details.

  • Q18. Can I leave special instructions for my order?

    Yes, you can leave special instructions for your order at checkout, such as any allergies or modifications you would like made to the dishes.

  • Q19. How do I set up my menu on Yazo Eats?

    You can set up your menu on Yazo Eats by logging into your restaurant account on our website. You will be able to add and manage items, prices, and descriptions for your menu.

  • Q20. How does Yazo Eats handle order fulfillment?

    Orders placed through Yazo Eats will be sent directly to your restaurant's kitchen for preparation. You will be responsible for fulfilling the orders by preparing and packaging the food for pickup.

  • Q21. Can I set pickup times for orders on Yazo Eats?

    Yes, you can set pickup times for orders on Yazo Eats by logging into your restaurant account on our website. You can also set a pickup time for each individual order at the time it is placed.

  • Q22. How do I track the status of orders on Yazo Eats?

    You can track the status of orders on Yazo Eats by logging into your restaurant account on our website. You will be able to view and update the status of all orders placed through Yazo Eats, including those that are currently being prepared and those that have been completed.

  • Q23. Can I offer promotions or discounts on Yazo Eats?

    Yes, you can offer promotions or discounts on Yazo Eats by logging into your restaurant account on our website. You can create and manage promotions and discounts for your menu items, and set expiration dates for them.

  • Q1. What is Yazo Clock?

    Yazo Clock is an employee time tracking system that allows businesses to track employee hours and manage employee schedules by clocking in with camera or manually.

  • Q2. How does Yazo Clock integrate with other systems?

    Yazo Clock can integrate with payroll systems, accounting software, and other employee management systems.

  • Q3. How does Yazo Clock improve employee management?

    Yazo Clock allows businesses to track employee hours, manage employee schedules, and monitor employee performance, making it easy to improve efficiency and productivity. Camera clock in makes sure it is the right employee logging in.

  • Q4. How do I set up Yazo Clock for my business?

    To set up Yazo Clock for your business, simply sign up for an account on our website, and follow the prompts to set up your business profile, add your employees, and set up their schedules.

  • Q5. How do I track my employee's hours using Yazo Clock?

    Employees can clock in and out using a selfie, pin or QR code, and managers can view employee's hours in real-time, including overtime and breaks.

  • Q6. How do I manage employee schedules using Yazo Clock?

    You can manage employee schedules by creating shifts, assigning employees to shifts, and adjusting schedules as needed. You can also track employee availability and request time off.

  • Q7. Can I track employee attendance and punctuality?

    Yes, Yazo Clock tracks employee attendance and punctuality, and allows managers to view employee's hours, late arrival or early departures, and overtime.

  • Q8. Is Yazo Clock compatible with other payroll or accounting software?

    Yes, Yazo Clock can integrate with a variety of payroll and accounting software, such as QuickBooks, Xero or similar.
